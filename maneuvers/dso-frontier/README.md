## OVERVIEW

This folder contains a complete, ready-to-execute Go-To-Market (GTM) package for ekwa.com's strategic entry into the 3–15 location Dental Support Organization (DSO) market segment.

**Opportunity:** $1.5M+ ARR from 40+ DSO clients within 18 months
**Market Gap:** Underserved segment between enterprise platforms ($10K–$25K/month) and single-practice agencies
**ekwa Moat:** 16+ years dental expertise, exclusivity model, human-centric approach

## HOW TO USE THIS GTM PACKAGE

### Phase 1: Internal Alignment (Week 1)
1. Review **campaign-brief.md** with leadership
   - Validate market opportunity ($1.5M ARR target)
   - Align on positioning & messaging
   - Confirm team structure & resource allocation
2. Kick off internal DSO team (sales, marketing, delivery)
3. Finalize pricing & contract terms

### Phase 2: Marketing & Web (Week 2–3)
1. Deploy **landing-page.html**
   - Host at dso.ekwa.com or ekwa.com/dso
   - Set up analytics tracking
   - Create short link for email outreach
2. Customize **proposal-template.md** with ekwa branding
   - Create master template in Word/Google Docs
   - Set up DocuSign or PDF signing
3. Prepare **email-outreach.md** sequence
   - Create 3 email templates in HubSpot/Gmail
   - Build prospect list (200+ contacts)
   - Schedule first batch (50 emails)

### Phase 3: Sales Preparation (Week 3–4)
1. Train sales team on messaging using **campaign-brief.md**
2. Create **roi-calculator.md** templates (Excel + sheets for each tier)
3. Brief team on partnership strategy (**partnerships-list.md**)
   - Assign partnership development responsibilities
   - Start outreach to Tier 1 partners

### Phase 4: Launch & Execution (Week 5–8)
1. **Sales outreach:** Deploy 50 cold emails (Email 1 of sequence)
2. **Partnership outreach:** Contact 5–6 Tier 1 DSO consultants
3. **Content:** Publish first thought leadership article (LinkedIn, Group Dentistry Now)
4. **Events:** Finalize ADSO Summit sponsorship (June 15–17)
5. **Follow-up:** Deploy Email 2 to warm prospects

### Phase 5: Results & Optimization (Week 9–12)
1. Follow up Email 3 (the offer with free audit)
2. Schedule strategy calls (target: 10–15 in 90 days)
3. Close first DSO clients (target: 1–2 proof-of-concept clients)
4. Refine messaging based on sales feedback
5. Plan ADSO Summit booth & presentation
