# Operation Exclusivity Lock™ — Campaign Brief
## Q1 2026 Amplify360 Displacement Campaign

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
**Objective:** Convert 25-35 dental practices currently using Amplify360 to ekwa's exclusive dental marketing platform within 90 days, generating $1.5M+ in annual recurring revenue (ARR).

**Core Insight:** Amplify360 serves competing practices in the same geographic market without exclusivity guarantees. Practice owners are unaware they're funding competitors' marketing through the same agency. This creates a unique vulnerability.

**Campaign Angle:** *"Your Marketing Agency is Also Working for Your Competitors"* — positioning ekwa's exclusivity guarantee as a competitive moat that Amplify360 fundamentally cannot match.

---

## SITUATION ANALYSIS

### Amplify360 Vulnerabilities
- **No Exclusivity Guarantee:** Serves 800+ dental practices with zero territorial restrictions. Competes directly against their own clients.
- **Merger Integration Risk:** SmartBox + Dental Revolution 2022 merger. Client bases consolidated but churn increased post-merger.
- **Templated Content Strategy:** Primarily PPC/paid ads focused. Generic dental templates, not specialty/practice-specific customization.
- **Commodity Positioning:** Priced at $1,500–$3,000/month. Fights on price, not differentiation. Higher churn rate documented.
- **Lack of Dental Deep Expertise:** Broader healthcare focus. Not dental-only like ekwa.

### ekwa's Competitive Advantages
- **16+ Years Dental Specialization:** Genuine expertise, not generalist healthcare agency.
- **Exclusivity Guarantee:** ONE client per specialty per geographic market. Practice owners know their agency doesn't work for competitors.
- **Human Expert Team:** No AI-generated templates. Custom content by dental specialists.
- **400+ Established Client Base:** Proof of longevity, retention, loyalty.
- **$2,500–$4,500/month Positioning:** Premium price justified by exclusivity + specialization.

### Market Opportunity
- **Target Universe:** ~800 Amplify360 practices
- **Addressable Market (90 days):** 150-200 practices in major metros where ekwa has capacity
- **Expected Conversion Rate:** 15-23% (based on similar B2B SaaS displacement campaigns)
- **Target Unit Economics:** 30 conversions × $3,000/month average = $90K/month = $1.08M ARR (conservative)

## CHANNEL STRATEGY

### Channel 1: LinkedIn Outreach (40% of budget)
**Rationale:** Dental practice owners actively use LinkedIn. B2B outreach at scale. Personalization possible at individual level.

**Tactics:**
- Identify Amplify360 client practices via LinkedIn (company pages, practice staff)
- Personalized connection requests + 3-message follow-up sequence
- Sponsored content targeting "dental practice owners" + "dentists" (US only, $50K+ company size)
- Conversion: Connection → Message Thread → Landing Page → CTA Form

**Budget Allocation:** $25,000
**Timeline:** Weeks 1–12 (ongoing)
**Expected Output:** 40–50 SQL, 8–12 conversions

---

### Channel 2: Direct Mail + Landing Page (25% of budget)
**Rationale:** High-touch, premium positioning. Stands out in crowded digital environment. Physical mail reads as more "legitimate" to older practice owners.

**Tactics:**
- Identify top 100 Amplify360 practices by market size + revenue potential
- Send custom postcard: "Is Your Marketing Agency Also Working for Your Competitors?" + personalized landing page URL
- Landing page: Practice-specific comparison (city-level competitive context)
- CTA: "Check if your market is still available" form + phone number

**Budget Allocation:** $15,000 (design, printing, postage for 1,000 pieces)
**Timeline:** Weeks 2–6 (send in batches)
**Expected Output:** 30–40 website visits, 5–8 SQL, 2–3 conversions

---

### Channel 3: Google Ads (Branded Keywords) (20% of budget)
**Rationale:** Capture intent. When practices search "Amplify360" reviews, pricing, complaints — they're in research/evaluation mode.

**Tactics:**
- Bid on "Amplify360," "Amplify360 reviews," "Amplify360 alternatives," "Amplify360 pricing"
- Ad copy: "Comparing Dental Marketing Agencies? See How ekwa's Exclusivity Works."
- Landing page: Direct comparison table vs. Amplify360
- Conversion: Ad → Landing Page → Form → Sales Call

**Budget Allocation:** $12,000 (12-week campaign, ~$3 CPC estimated)
**Timeline:** Weeks 1–12
**Expected Output:** 30–40 clicks to LP, 3–5 SQL, 1–2 conversions

---

### Channel 4: Email Outreach (Direct) (10% of budget)
**Rationale:** Low cost, high personalization possible. Complements LinkedIn outreach.

**Tactics:**
- Obtain email list of Amplify360 clients via ZoomInfo, Apollo, or manual research
- 5-email sequence (see Email Sequence deliverable)
- Staggered sends: Days 1, 4, 8, 14, 21
- Conversion: Email → Click → Landing Page → Form → Sales Call

**Budget Allocation:** $8,000 (email platform, list building, copywriting)
**Timeline:** Weeks 2–10
**Expected Output:** 25–30 SQL, 4–6 conversions

---

### Channel 5: Retargeting + Organic (5% of budget)
**Rationale:** Reinforce messaging to warm prospects. Build brand recall.

**Tactics:**
- Website pixel on landing page visitors
- Retarget with display ads: "Still thinking about switching? Let's talk."
- Organic content: SEO blog posts on "exclusivity in dental marketing," "non-exclusive agencies," "marketing conflicts of interest"
- Conversion: Organic search + warm retargeting → Landing Page → CTA

**Budget Allocation:** $3,000
**Timeline:** Weeks 4–12
**Expected Output:** 10–15 SQL, 2–3 conversions

## RISK MITIGATION & CONTINGENCIES

### Risk 1: Low Email Deliverability
**Mitigation:** Use verified email list from ZoomInfo/Apollo. Warm up sending domains. Monitor bounce rates weekly.

### Risk 2: Amplify360 Retaliatory Pricing
**Mitigation:** Emphasize exclusivity, not price. Position as premium. If price war emerges, highlight switching costs vs. long-term value.

### Risk 3: LinkedIn Connection Fatigue
**Mitigation:** Vary message timing, personalize heavily, include valuable content (not just sales).

### Risk 4: Low Landing Page Conversion
**Mitigation:** A/B test headlines, CTAs, social proof. Ensure mobile responsiveness. Include trust signals (16 years, 400+ clients).

### Risk 5: Sales Team Capacity
**Mitigation:** Pre-qualify MQL heavily. Create chatbot or auto-response to qualify before human handoff.
