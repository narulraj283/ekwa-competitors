Ekwa's Top 5 Differentiators for Chiropractic

1. Enterprise SEO for Chiro Clinics

Most chiropractic marketing is low-budget template sites. Ekwa brings enterprise infrastructure—100+ P1 keywords, condition-specific content (back pain, neck pain, sciatica, whiplash), technical optimization—to the chiropractic space.

2. Retention Strategy Built In

Chiropractic is about recurring patients. Ekwa's content targets retention—wellness content, ongoing care protocols, patient education on spinal health, and protocols for recurring visits. Not just acquiring first-time patients.

3. 30-50 New Patient/Month Optimization

Average chiropractor needs 30–50 new patients/month to sustain growth. Ekwa's strategy targets that range. We guarantee 20+ calls/month minimum, which converts to 15–30 new patient exams depending on your practice.

4. Done-For-You vs. Platform DIY

Perfect Patients, ChiroMatrix, and others are platforms—you manage them. Ekwa is done-for-you—we create content, manage SEO, handle call tracking, and own your strategy. You focus on treating patients, not managing dashboards.

5. $0 Custom Chiropractic Website

200 hours of custom chiro website design included free. Condition-specific pages, patient education flows, appointment booking optimization, and conversion-focused design. Worth $10K–$20K elsewhere. Ekwa absorbs it to win the partnership.

Bonus: Geographic Exclusivity

One Ekwa partner per metro market. Your competitors in the same city don't have access to Ekwa. You get undivided focus and can dominate locally without competition from other practices.

Discovery Questions (10 Essential Questions)

1
How many new patient exams are you scheduling per month right now? Is that growing or flat?
2
What's your average patient lifetime value, and how many visits do they typically complete?
3
Where are your new patients coming from? Referrals, Google, insurance networks, or other?
4
How much are you currently spending on marketing, and what channels are you using?
5
What's your biggest frustration with your current marketing partner or platform?
6
Are you using Perfect Patients, ChiroMatrix, or another platform? How hands-on are you with managing it?
7
What conditions do you focus on? (back pain, neck pain, sports injuries, wellness) How does that affect your marketing?
8
What would a 40% increase in new patient flow mean for your annual revenue?
9
Are you willing to commit to a dedicated partnership (geographic exclusivity), or do you prefer flexibility?
10
What's your timeline to see ROI, and how are you measuring marketing success today?
