Ekwa's Top 5 Differentiators for Concierge Medicine

1. Marketing Infrastructure from Zero

Most DPC/concierge practices launch with zero marketing infrastructure. Ekwa builds everything from scratch—brand, website, content, patient acquisition engine, retention strategy. No competitor does this.

2. Membership Model Revenue Optimization

Concierge medicine targets $150–$500/month per patient (very high LTV). Ekwa optimizes for filling your panel (300–600 patient target) and driving long-term retention, not quick sales. Different game.

3. 100+ Keywords Page 1 Guarantee

Written guarantee: 100+ concierge/DPC-related keywords ranking on Google Page 1. Keywords like "direct primary care," "concierge medicine," "membership medicine," "DPC benefits." Competitors offer no such commitment.

4. 20+ Monthly Membership Inquiries Guaranteed

Minimum 20+ qualified calls per month. These are patients interested in membership medicine, not generic healthcare traffic. Most convert to sign-ups at 40–60% rate = 8–12 new members/month.

5. $0 Concierge Medicine Website

200 hours of custom website design built specifically for membership medicine. Pricing pages, membership benefits explainers, physician bio optimization, patient testimonials, membership sign-up flows. Worth $15K–$25K elsewhere. Ekwa includes it free.

Bonus: DPC-Specialized Content

Ekwa creates content around "concierge medicine benefits," "DPC vs. insurance," "membership medicine value," and "direct primary care model"—topics most physicians don't rank for. We own this space for you.
