No-Show Sequence

2-email sequence for missed or cancelled scheduled meetings

Sequence Overview

Goal: Address the miss with minimal friction, acknowledge their busy schedule, and offer alternative times to reschedule.

Timeline: Same day (Day 0), Day 3

Best for: Prospects who scheduled a call but didn't show or cancelled without follow-up.

Day 0 (Same Day)
No worries, {{contact.firstname}} — let's reschedule
Preview: "Completely understand you're busy"

Hi {{contact.firstname}},

No stress at all — I know running {{contact.company}} is demanding, and sometimes calls slip.

I was really looking forward to diving into how we can help {{contact.company}} attract the right patient mix — both cosmetic and medical derm — but let's just pick a different time.

How does next week look? I'm flexible on timing and format (call, email overview, whatever works best for you).

— {{owner.firstname}}

Day 3
Still interested in talking, {{contact.firstname}}?
Preview: "Just want to make sure this is still a priority"

Hi {{contact.firstname}},

I haven't heard back on rescheduling, so I wanted to check in — is this still something you want to explore, or has priority shifted for {{contact.company}} right now?

Either way is fine. If you're interested but just need more flexibility, I can send you a brief overview of how we help dermatology practices position themselves for the patient types that matter most — cosmetic, medical, or both.

If it's not the right time, I'll stay in touch and check back in a few months.

Just let me know.

— {{owner.firstname}}
