Provider Switch Sequence

3-email sequence for prospects actively switching from their current marketing provider

Sequence Overview

Goal: Position Ekwa as the specialist alternative to generalist marketing agencies. Emphasize dermatology-specific expertise and the risks of switching to another generalist provider.

Timeline: Day 1, Day 5, Day 10

Best for: Prospects who've mentioned wanting to leave their current provider or are actively evaluating alternatives.

Key Context: Dermatologists often partner with large generalist agencies that don't understand the unique differences between cosmetic and medical dermatology marketing.

Day 1
Making a switch? Here's what I'd reconsider

Day 5
Why other dermatology practices chose Ekwa

Day 10
Let's talk about timing on this switch
