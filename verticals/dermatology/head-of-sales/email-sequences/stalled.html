Stalled Deal Sequence

3-email sequence for deals that have gone quiet after initial engagement

Sequence Overview

Goal: Reactivate the conversation by uncovering hidden objections, providing new value, and creating urgency around missing the opportunity window.

Timeline: Day 1, Day 7, Day 21

Best for: Prospects who were engaged (had calls, received proposals) but haven't responded in 7+ days.

Day 1
Checking in on {{contact.company}} — {{contact.firstname}}
Preview: "Haven't heard from you in a bit"

Hi {{contact.firstname}},

It's been a minute since we last connected about the dermatology marketing strategy for {{contact.company}}. I wanted to check in and see where things stand.

Sometimes deals stall because there's an objection that didn't come up in our conversation — maybe it's budget, timeline, or uncertainty about how specialized dermatology marketing actually moves the needle on patient acquisition.

All of that is totally normal, and I'd rather address it head-on than have it slow things down.

What's the biggest question or concern on your end right now?

— {{owner.firstname}}

Day 7
One more thing on dermatology patient acquisition
Preview: "Something that came up with a similar practice"

Hi {{contact.firstname}},

I was working with a derm practice similar to {{contact.company}} yesterday, and something they said stuck with me. They were concerned that switching from their current marketing partner would disrupt patient flow — but the reality is, they were already losing momentum under the old strategy.

Three months into working with Ekwa, they're not just back on track — they're seeing 40% more qualified inquiries for cosmetic procedures and 25% more booked medical derm consultations.

The window to capture spring and summer patient demand is now. If {{contact.company}} is serious about growth, the time to act is this month, not next quarter.

I'm happy to jump on a quick call and walk through exactly what that looks like. No pressure — but I think it's worth 15 minutes of your time.

— {{owner.firstname}}

Day 21
{{contact.firstname}} — final outreach on this
Preview: "Before I move on"

Hi {{contact.firstname}},

I realize it's been a few weeks since we talked, and I want to respect your time — so this is my last note on this before I circle back in a few months.

The truth is, we work best with practices that are ready to commit to growth now. If {{contact.company}} isn't there yet, that's completely okay. But if you're on the fence because of questions or concerns, I don't want you to miss out on what's possible.

One final offer: I can put together a detailed patient acquisition roadmap specifically for {{contact.company}} — 90-day strategy, projected results for both cosmetic and medical derm segments, and exact investment. It'll take me about an hour, and it's yours to evaluate however you want. No follow-up needed unless you're ready to move forward.

If that's helpful, just let me know. Otherwise, best of luck with {{contact.company}}, and I'll be in touch in the fall.

— {{owner.firstname}}
