# Ophthalmology Email Sequences - Head of Sales

Professional email sequence templates for the Ophthalmology vertical, designed for sales engagement and lead nurturing.

## Files Created

### 1. **index.html**
Hub page featuring all 5 email sequences with navigation and sequence cards.
- Responsive grid layout
- Quick access to all sequences
- Professional branding with #40916C accent color

### 2. **lead-nurture.html** 
Initial engagement sequence for warm leads (3 emails, Days 1/3/7)
- Day 1: Opens conversation about dual-patient acquisition challenge
- Day 3: Provides market data and validation (40-60% increase stats)
- Day 7: Soft close asking for 15-minute call

**Key Focus:** Ophthalmology-specific challenge—attracting both LASIK patients (emotional, high-value) and routine eye care patients (insurance-driven)

### 3. **post-msm.html**
Follow-up after marketing strategy meetings (4 emails, Days 1/2/4/7)
- Day 1: Meeting recap with next steps
- Day 2: Case study with specific results
- Day 4: Quantifies their stated goals and moves to strategy session
- Day 7: Invites deeper engagement with market-specific strategy session

**Key Focus:** Reinforces discussion points and drives progress toward implementation

### 4. **no-show.html**
Reengagement after missed meetings (2 emails, Days 1/3)
- Day 1: Empathetic acknowledgment, reschedule attempt
- Day 3: Final value proposition and soft door-open

**Key Focus:** Non-blaming, easy reschedule path without friction

### 5. **stalled.html**
Re-activation for deals that have gone quiet (3 emails, Days 1/3/7)
- Day 1: Friendly check-in with multiple possible reasons
- Day 3: Addresses "good enough current provider" objection
- Day 7: Final closing email keeping door open for future

**Key Focus:** Differentiates specialized approach from generic solutions

### 6. **provider-switch.html**
Targeted sequence for EHR-bundled solution users (3 emails, Days 1/4/8)
- Day 1: Opens with core insight—EHR solutions are adequate but not specialized
- Day 4: Provides side-by-side comparison of generic vs. specialized approaches
- Day 8: Invites strategy session with specific value proposition

**Key Focus:** Emphasizes specialized ophthalmology content, procedure-specific SEO, and physician credentialing advantages

## Features

All pages include:
- Professional responsive design
- Copy-to-clipboard buttons for all email content
- HubSpot token support ({{contact.firstname}}, {{contact.company}}, {{user.firstname}}, etc.)
- Ekwa brand colors and styling
- Navigation between sequences
- Footer with back-to-home link
- Mobile-friendly layout

## Key Guidelines Implemented

- NO competitor names (no "EyeCarePro", "EyeMDPro", "iMatrix", "Tebra")
- Uses "your current provider" instead
- Focuses on ophthalmology-specific challenges:
  - Dual patient acquisition (LASIK vs. routine)
  - Procedure-specific SEO positioning
  - Physician credentialing emphasis
  - Medical sophistication in positioning
- Accent color: #40916C (Ekwa green)
- CSS import: `<link rel="stylesheet" href="../../../../assets/css/ekwa-brand.css">`

3. Email timing is guideline—adjust based on sales velocity and lead quality
4. Notes sections provide context for why each email works
5. All pages maintain consistent branding and navigation
