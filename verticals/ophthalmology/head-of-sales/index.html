Email Sequences

Five core sequences to deploy across the ophthalmology sales funnel:

Sequence 1: First Contact

Trigger: New contact tagged "Ophthalmology"

Goal: Establish relationship, qualify practice type and pain point

Typical cadence: Emails over 5-7 days

Sequence 2: Post-Demo Follow-up

Trigger: Deal stage = "Demo Completed"

Goal: Recap demo, address objections, move to proposal

Sequence 3: Proposal Sent

Trigger: Deal stage = "Proposal Sent"

Goal: Set clear expectations, timeline, and next steps

Typical cadence: Emails over 7-10 days

Sequence 4: Stalled Deal Re-engagement

Goal: Unblock deal, discover true objection, re-engage decision maker

Typical cadence: Emails over 5-7 days

Sequence 5: Closed Lost Learning

Trigger: Deal stage = "Closed Lost"

Goal: Gather feedback, build relationships, stay in nurture pool

Typical cadence: 1-2 emails over 14 days

Email Sequence Details

Sequence 1: First Contact Outreach ‚ñº

Objective: Introduce Ekwa, establish credibility, qualify practice type and pain point

Email 1 (Day 0): "Helping [Practice Name] Grow with Ekwa"

  • Subject: "Helping ophthalmology practices grow [Company Name]"
  • Hook: Mention specific challenges (patient acquisition, online visibility, scheduling)
  • CTA: "Let's chat for 15 minutes next week"

Email 2 (Day 2): Practice Type Qualification

  • Subject: "Quick question about your practice [Company Name]"
  • Hook: Ask about their specific focus (General, LASIK, Retina, etc.)
  • CTA: "Reply with your focus area so I can send relevant resources"

Email 3 (Day 4): Value Proposition (Practice-Specific)

  • Subject: "ROI case study: [Practice Type]"
  • Hook: Share specific win story (LASIK clinic grew by X%, retina specialist improved patient retention by Y%)
  • CTA: "Schedule a 20-min demo"

Email 4 (Day 6): Gentle Nudge (No Response)

  • Subject: "Following up: ophthalmology growth resources"
  • Hook: Brief recap, add new angle (patient reviews, scheduling efficiency)
  • CTA: "Let me know best time to chat"

Email 5 (Day 7): Final Soft Close

  • Subject: "One more thing before I stop reaching out"
  • Hook: Exclusive resource or free audit offer
  • CTA: "Claim your free audit or let me know if not a fit right now"
Sequence 2: Post-Demo Follow-up ‚ñº

Objective: Recap demo, address objections, move to proposal and negotiation

Email 1 (Day 0): Demo Recap + Next Steps

  • Subject: "Great meeting today ‚Äî here's what's next"
  • Hook: Personalized recap of demo, 2-3 key features discussed
  • Include: Timeline to proposal, next meeting date
  • CTA: "Reply if you have any follow-up questions"

Email 2 (Day 2): Objection Handling Email

  • Subject: "Addressing [common objection for their practice type]"
  • Hook: Proactively address pricing, integration, timeline, ROI concerns
  • Include: FAQ, resources, customer testimonials
  • CTA: "Schedule a brief 15-min call to dive deeper"

Email 3 (Day 4): Proposal Sent

  • Subject: "Your custom proposal is ready for [Practice Name]"
  • Hook: Recap pricing, onboarding timeline, support tier
  • Include: Pricing breakout, ROI projection, success metrics
  • CTA: "Review and let's discuss any adjustments"

Email 4 (Day 5): Check-in (Proposal Pending)

  • Subject: "Any questions about your proposal [Practice Name]?"
  • Hook: Soft check-in, offer to schedule Q&A call
  • CTA: "Let me know if you'd like to discuss or need any edits"
Sequence 3: Proposal Sent & Negotiation ‚ñº

Objective: Set clear expectations, manage timeline, move to close

Email 1 (Day 0): Proposal Delivered

  • Subject: "Your customized Ekwa proposal for [Practice Name]"
  • Hook: Summarize pricing, value, onboarding timeline
  • Include: Next steps checklist, decision timeline
  • CTA: "Review and schedule a 20-min review call"

Email 2 (Day 3): 72-Hour Review Checkpoint

  • Subject: "Quick question on your proposal review"
  • Hook: Soft nudge, offer to clarify any line items
  • CTA: "Let me know if you have questions or need adjustments"

Email 3 (Day 6): Negotiation Support

  • Subject: "Let's discuss your proposal adjustments"
  • Hook: If they've raised questions, address them directly
  • Include: Flexibility options (payment terms, add-ons, discount thresholds)
  • CTA: "Schedule 15-min call to finalize"

Email 4 (Day 8): Final Push to Close

  • Subject: "Let's get you onboarded this month [Practice Name]"
  • Hook: Emphasize benefit of early start, outline onboarding
  • Include: Contract, next meeting scheduled
  • CTA: "Sign and reply to lock in your start date"

Email 5 (Day 10): Contract Signature Follow-up

  • Subject: "One signature away from launch"
  • Hook: Make signing easy, provide direct DocuSign link
  • CTA: "Click to sign and we'll send onboarding details"
Sequence 4: Stalled Deal Re-engagement ‚ñº

Objective: Identify blockers, re-engage decision maker, unfreeze stalled deals

Email 1 (Day 0): Gentle Check-in

  • Subject: "Checking in: anything we can clarify [Practice Name]?"
  • Hook: Acknowledge silence respectfully, ask directly about blockers
  • CTA: "Reply with any questions or concerns"

Email 2 (Day 2): Objection Uncovering

  • Subject: "Common questions from ophthalmology practices like yours"
  • Hook: List FAQs about pricing, ROI, implementation, integration
  • Include: Case study from similar practice
  • CTA: "Which of these is your biggest concern?"

Email 3 (Day 4): Decision Maker Escalation

  • Subject: "Brief conversation with your practice owner [Name]?"
  • Hook: Politely request to speak with final decision maker
  • Include: Value prop, testimonial from similar practice type
  • CTA: "Let me know best time for a 15-min call with the owner"

Email 4 (Day 6): Special Offer (Time-Bound)

  • Subject: "Special pricing if we close this week [Practice Name]"
  • Hook: Create urgency with limited-time offer
  • Include: Discount % or add-on value, end date
  • CTA: "Take advantage by end of week"

Email 5 (Day 7): Final Soft Close or Nurture

  • Subject: "Pausing our conversation ‚Äî but let's stay connected"
  • Hook: Respect their decision, offer to stay in nurture
  • Include: Add to periodic industry updates/case study newsletter
  • CTA: "Let me know if situation changes or if I can help later"
Sequence 5: Closed Lost Learning & Nurture ‚ñº

Objective: Gather feedback, maintain relationship, nurture for future opportunities

Email 1 (Day 0): Loss Feedback Request

  • Subject: "Quick feedback: why didn't we win your business?"
  • Hook: Ask for honest feedback on loss reason
  • Include: 3-4 multiple choice options (budget, timing, feature gap, chose competitor)
  • CTA: "Reply with which one applies ‚Äî this helps us improve"

Email 2 (Day 7): Nurture & Stay Connected

  • Subject: "[Practice Name], let's stay in touch"
  • Hook: Acknowledge loss professionally, offer value
  • Include: New case study, upcoming webinar, relevant resource
  • CTA: "Subscribe to our monthly ophthalmology insights"

Follow-up Strategy:

  • Move contact to "Ophthalmology ‚Äî Nurture" list
  • Add to monthly case study, industry insights, competitor intelligence emails
  • Re-engage every 3-6 months with new features, pricing updates, success stories
  • Mark for re-outreach at 12-month mark or when triggers appear (new hire, acquisition, feature announcement)
