Market Overview & Opportunities

Market Insight: The ophthalmology digital marketing sector is dominated by 2-3 specialists (EyeCarePro, EyeMDPro) creating a clear positioning gap. The $77.1B global market with 6.4% CAGR, combined with high-value procedures (LASIK $4K–$6K, cataract $3K–$6K per case) and the dry eye epidemic creating recurring $500–$2,000/year revenue per patient, makes this a premium client opportunity for ekwa.

Top 3 Procedure Opportunities

LASIK Resurgence

Trend: Post-COVID LASIK demand surged 20%+. Freedom-seeking patients + new SMILE technology = new patient acquisition wave.

LTV: $4,000–$6,000 per case. Content-driven conversions (candidacy, technology comparison, surgeon selection) are high-ROI.

Dry Eye Epidemic

Trend: 16M+ diagnosed Americans. Screen time explosion = Lipiflow, IPL, Restasis, Xiidra demand spike.

Recurring Revenue: $500–$2,000/year per patient. Patients return 4–8 times/year. Best for practice LTV expansion.

Premium IOL Upsell

Trend: Cataract surgery with toric/multifocal IOLs creates $2,000–$4,000 out-of-pocket upsell per case.

Conversion Driver: Patient education content on IOL options drives premium upgrade. High-margin, high-conversion opportunity.

Market Segments

Refractive Surgery Specialists

LASIK/PRK/SMILE-focused practices. High marketing spend ($6K–$12K/mo), high case value ($4K–$6K). Direct acquisition focus.

Cataract Surgery Centers

Volume-based + premium IOL upsell. Marketing drives premium IOL education. $3K–$6K per case including surgical center revenue.

Comprehensive Practices

OD + ophtho combined. Medical ophthalmology (glaucoma, retina co-management) + surgical. Complex multi-service-line marketing.

Multi-Specialty Eye Centers

Ophthalmology + optometry + optical retail + ASC under one roof. Fastest-growing segment. Requires unified marketing strategy.
