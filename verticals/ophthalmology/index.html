üéØ Playbook 1: The Exclusivity Pitch
Goal: Position Ekwa as the single strategic partner for ophthalmology growth.
  • Opening Move: "Every other vendor you work with can also serve your competitor across the street. We're different. We exclusively partner with one ophthalmology practice per market."
  • Proof Point: Show how their current 3-4 vendors all serve multiple competitors in their ZIP code (get data from web, LinkedIn, vendor disclosures)
  • The Offer: "We'll be YOUR dedicated partner, not a commoditized service. That means 100% of our innovation, resources, and strategy is optimized for YOUR success."
  • Contract Lock: 3-year minimum with annual review; built-in performance bonuses to ensure alignment
  • Success Metric: Win 1 practice per geography; 95%+ retention
üí° Playbook 2: The Category Authority Play
Goal: Become the voice/advisor for ophthalmology-specific challenges.
  • Content Strategy: Publish ophthalmology-specific case studies, benchmarks, and insights (not generic marketing advice)
  • Examples: "How refractive surgery centers market premium procedures" / "Insurance pre-auth appeal strategies for cataract surgery" / "Patient retention for routine vs. surgical practices"
  • Speaking/Events: Sponsor ophthalmology conferences (ASCRS, AAO) to build thought leadership
  • Sales Messaging: "We don't do generic marketing. We specialize in ophthalmology. Ask us about refractive surgery marketing or cataract surgery patient acquisition."
  • Success Metric: 5 published case studies; 2+ speaking engagements; 30%+ of inbound leads from ophthalmology-specific search terms
üîß Playbook 3: The Technology Moat Narrative
Goal: Win on platform capability, AI, and automation vs. low-tech competitors.
  • Messaging: "Other agencies do manual work. We've built AI-driven automation for ophthalmology growth (patient identification, campaign optimization, reporting)."
  • Demo Strategy: Show live dashboard; demonstrate predictive analytics for patient acquisition ROI
  • Proof Points: "We've reduced campaign setup time by 70% and optimized CPL by 40% using proprietary algorithms"
  • Product Roadmap: Publicly share roadmap for new ophthalmology features; show momentum
  • Success Metric: Win 60%+ of comparisons with EyeMDPro, Specialty Vision, older competitors; strong NPS on "platform" dimension
üí∞ Playbook 4: The Mid-Market Sweet Spot
Goal: Capture mid-market ophthalmology practices underserved by enterprise giants.
  • Target Profile: 2-5 location ophthalmology groups with $2-10M in annual revenue (too big for small agencies, too small for Tebra/DoctorLogic minimums)
  • Value Prop: "You get enterprise-grade strategy and tech at a price that doesn't require you to be a $100M practice."
  • Bundled Offering: Package services (digital marketing + advisory + tech) at 30-40% discount vs. EyeCarePro or Tebra but with higher margins for Ekwa
  • Retention Play: Lock in multi-year contracts; make switching costs high
  • Success Metric: Secure 30+ mid-market clients; 50%+ margin; 90%+ retention
ü§ù Playbook 5: Partner Ecosystem Play
Goal: Build partnerships with complementary vendors (not direct competitors).
  • Partner Targets: EHR vendors (Epic, Allscripts), practice management software (Compulink, Open Dental), supply chain (Cardinal), telemedicine platforms
  • Opportunity: Become their "go-to marketing partner" for ophthalmology customers; referral revenue
  • Integration Strategy: Build lightweight integrations (API, single sign-on) to increase stickiness
  • Revenue Model: Referral fees + revenue share on growth services sold through partner channels
  • Success Metric: 5 active partnerships; 20%+ revenue from indirect channels
