12 Discovery Questions (Qualification & Objection Anticipation)

Practice Profile (Qualification)

1. What are your top 3 revenue-driving procedures?

Helps identify focus areas for content and PPC strategy. LASIK practices need different messaging than cataract practices.

2. How many procedures per month are you currently doing, and what's your average case value?

Establishes baseline for ROI calculation and growth potential.

3. Are you a solo practice, small group (2‚Äì3 surgeons), or larger group/multi-specialty center?

Determines which maneuver (Vision Lock, Eye Authority, Vision Group) is most relevant.

Current Marketing (Objection Anticipation)

4. Who are you currently using for marketing, and what are you paying?

Identifies competitor and pricing context. Anticipates "We're happy with EyeCarePro" objections early.

5. What's working well with your current marketing, and what's not?

Uncovers pain points. Look for comments like "We don't rank for LASIK," "Our competitors seem to get more leads," "Our agency doesn't understand ophthalmology specifics."

6. Are you losing patients to competitors, or are new patients just harder to find?

Differentiates between market saturation and poor marketing execution. Changes strategy priority.

Strategic Goals (Maneuver Alignment)

7. In the next 12 months, what's your primary growth goal? (New procedures, market expansion, service line growth?)

Aligns strategy. If they say "Expand LASIK volume," Eye Authority is the fit. If "Consolidate multi-location marketing," Vision Group is the fit.

8. Do you have a dry eye program or are you thinking about starting one?

Identifies recurring revenue opportunity. Dry eye = $500‚Äì$2K per patient annually. Major LTV expansion driver.

9. For cataract practices: What % of cataract patients are upgrading to premium IOLs?

Identifies premium IOL upsell opportunity. Good practices: 25‚Äì35% premium IOL adoption. Great practices: 40‚Äì50%. Content-driven upsell education can move this needle significantly.

Investment & Timeline (Closing Questions)

10. What's your current annual marketing budget, and are you open to adjusting it?

Qualifies budget. If they say "$2K/month max," they're not a Vision Group fit (which is $3.4K‚Äì$4.6K). But they might be iMatrix holdovers ready to upgrade.

11. If we could show ROI of X% in 6 months, would you be ready to commit to a 12-month engagement?

Tests receptiveness to contract model. Red flag if they refuse any commitment; suggests they're shopping or have vendor trust issues.

12. What would need to happen for you to switch from your current agency?

Final objection surface. Listen carefully. "Faster execution," "More strategy," "Better results" = signals you can win. "Lower price" = hard sell, competitor is iMatrix-like or they're price-sensitized.
