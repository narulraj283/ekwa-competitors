Ekwa's Top 5 Differentiators for Orthodontics

1. Ortho-Specific Long-Form Content

Ekwa creates 3,000–5,000 word clinical articles on braces vs. Invisalign, teen vs. adult orthodontics, cost transparency pages, treatment timelines, and retention protocols. DoctorLogic and OrthoFi give you generic templates. We give you content engines.

2. Case Value Revenue Modeling

Ortho practices average $5K–$8K case value with 15–30 cases/month. Ekwa models marketing around case conversion and case acceptance, not just patient flow. We optimize for revenue, not vanity metrics.

3. 100+ Keywords Page 1 Guarantee

Written guarantee: 100+ orthodontics-related keywords on Google Page 1. DoctorLogic and OrthoFi give you tools. Ekwa gives you results, backed in writing. Benchmark: 328 keywords P1 (Kings Dental).

4. 20+ Monthly Patient Calls Guaranteed

Minimum 20+ new patient calls per month, guaranteed. No impressions, no clicks, no vanity metrics—actual calls your practice can convert. Most Ekwa clients exceed this by 2–3x.

5. $0 Custom Orthodontics Website

200 hours of custom ortho website design and build, included free with every partnership. Patient journey flows, treatment explainers, cost transparency pages, before-and-afters gallery. Worth $10K–$20K elsewhere. Ekwa absorbs it to win the partnership.

Bonus: Geographic Exclusivity

One Ekwa partner per metro market. Your competitors in Austin, Denver, Phoenix—they don't have access to Ekwa. You get undivided focus and can dominate locally.
