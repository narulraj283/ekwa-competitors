Re-engagement sequence for opportunities that have gone quiet

Total Emails
3
Timeline
30 Days
Best For
Quiet Leads
Goal
Re-engage
Strategy Note: This sequence uses an extended cadence (30 days) because plastic surgery partnerships involve high-stakes decisions. Surgeons may be evaluating multiple options, consulting with partners, or simply busy with patient care. Stay top-of-mind without being pushy.
