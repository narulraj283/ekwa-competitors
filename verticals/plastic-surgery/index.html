Key Market Insights

  • GLP-1 Wave: Ozempic/Wegovy exploding creates 2026–2030 mega-trend for body contouring. First-mover content advantage critical.
  • Instagram/TikTok Dominance: Before/after galleries and surgeon celebrities driving patient acquisition for surgeons <45 years old.
  • Medical Tourism Pressure: International competition forcing domestic surgeons to compete on trust, credentials, and digital narrative.
  • Group Practice Consolidation: Private equity and PE-backed groups consolidating cosmetic surgery — larger, more sophisticated marketing budgets.
  • Highest Spend Vertical: Plastic surgery practices invest $5,000–$20,000/month on marketing. 3-5x higher spend than most medical specialties.
