Why Practices Switch Away from Cardinal

Cardinal is a full-service healthcare agency. Your account manager also handles dental practices, dermatology clinics, and plastic surgeries. When you need a quick decision or custom vet-specific strategy, you're competing for attention with their medical and dental clients. At ekwa, veterinary is our primary focus. Reason 2: Large Agency = Slower Response

Cardinal's case study (Southern Vet Partners: 54% lead increase) proves they can deliver results. But with 400+ animal hospital clients, your account is one of many. Strategy changes, content revisions, campaign tweaks — they take longer to execute at scale. Growing practices need faster decisions. Reason 3: Limited Vet-Specific Strategy

Cardinal applies healthcare marketing best practices to veterinary. But veterinary is unique. Fear Free certification marketing, AAHA positioning, pet parent community building — these aren't standard healthcare strategies. ekwa's entire playbook is built around veterinary-specific differentiation. Our case studies feature practices using Fear Free and certification positioning to out-market corporate chains, not just generic lead generation.
